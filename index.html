Welcome to the Furniture Finance Challenge

In this game you will design a room while managing a limited budget.

Your first home as a young adult often includes a mix of secondhand furniture and less expensive new items.

As you grow older and your home gets larger, you may upgrade your furniture and add new pieces.

Well-made furniture usually costs more but can last many years.

Buying preowned furniture can be a good way to get higher quality items at a lower price.

Less expensive furniture can still be stylish, but it may not last as long.

Large furniture purchases are often bought on credit and paid off with monthly payments.

Fun Fact: In 2018, the most searched furniture items in the United States were a sectional couch and a TV stand.

Now roll the dice to get your budget and start designing!
